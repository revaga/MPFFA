Shubha Kashinath

Shubha Kashinath is a parent of rising seniors at MSJHS. She has been an engaged member of MPPFA for the past 3 years. Prior to serving on the Executive Board, she chaired the Parent Advocacy Committee. She brings over 20 years of experience in higher education as a professor of speech language pathology at Cal State East Bay. She is passionate about supporting families and teachers in creating equitable and inclusive learning environments for all students.
